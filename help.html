How to Help

There are many ways you can help us with our mission to save whale sharks! Read more below about how you can protect the ocean's gentle giants.

Make a Donation

Donations to The Gentle Giants Project help to fund our research program, habitat conservation efforts, and educational events.

Adopt a Whale Shark

Through our adoption program, you can name and keep track of your own whale shark while also supporting our research efforts. Every $50 adoption comes with an adoption certificate and monthly updates from our research team!

Attend an Event

Every year, we host a variety of events with the goal to spread awareness of the whale shark species, the threats they face, and the work we do to help them. We update our events section regularly to showcase our upcoming events, so check back often!

Earth Day Clean Up

This year, we are coming together on Earth Day (April 22nd) to clean up our local beach. Attendance is free, and refreshments will be available along with a presentation on effect of pollution on whale sharks and their habitats.

The Tourist Report

Join us Saturday, May 2nd for a guest lecture by marine biologist Lucy Smith to learn more about the impact of tourism on ocean habitats. Tickets are $15, and the talk starts at 5pm with an opportunity for questions at the end.

Poster Competition

Over the summer, we are hosting a youth poster competition. Kids aged 5-8, 9-11, 12-14, and 15-18 can submit a poster design about whale sharks and their conservation. The winner from each category will receive a prize pack along with having their poster displayed on our website and in several aquariums across the country!
