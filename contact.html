FAQs

The funds we receive from our supporters go towards our research program, habitat conservation efforts, and funding future educational events.

The Gentle Giants Project focuses all of its time and resources on protecting whale sharks. However, our research and habitat work has positive effects for other species that share the ocean with whale sharks. We also partner with other marine conservation organizations that help other animals frequently.

The best way to keep up to date with The Gentle Giants Project is to follow us on social media where we regularly post about our research, the whales we are tracking, and our upcoming events. We also keep our website up-to-date with our progress and events.
